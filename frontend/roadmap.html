Development Roadmap

Track the progress of Consensus Builder features from conception to completion. This roadmap shows what's been built, what's in development, and what's planned for the future.

Oct 2024
Basic Parcel Visualization
Interactive map with parcel boundaries, clickable parcels showing detailed information including area, estimated market price, and geometric analysis.
Completed
Nov 2024
Road Detection & Management
Automatic road detection from OpenStreetMap data, manual road drawing tools, road width analysis, and integration with parcel block formation.
Completed
Nov 2024
Parcel Block Formation
Algorithm to automatically group parcels into blocks based on road boundaries, with manual block management and building placement capabilities.
Completed
Dec 2024
Proposal System
Complete proposal creation and management system with multi-parcel selection, proposal visualization, storage, and listing functionality.
Completed
Dec 2024
Measurement Tools
Interactive measurement tool for calculating distances on the map with precise HTRS96 coordinate system calculations.
Completed
Jan 2025
Enhanced Building Management
Improved building placement algorithms, building type selection, and automatic building optimization for parcel blocks.
In Progress
Feb 2025
Proposal Sharing & Export
Share proposals via URL, export to PDF/image formats, and generate detailed proposal reports with cost analysis and visual previews.
Planned
Mar 2025
Community Voting System
Allow community members to vote on proposals, comment on designs, and reach consensus through structured decision-making processes.
Planned
Apr 2025
Advanced Analytics
Detailed analytics dashboard showing development impact, cost analysis, traffic flow simulation, and environmental impact assessment.
Planned
May 2025
Mobile Application
Native mobile apps for iOS and Android with offline capabilities, GPS integration, and field data collection features.
Planned
Jun 2025
Multi-User Collaboration
Real-time collaborative editing, user accounts, permissions system, and synchronized proposal development across multiple users.
Planned
