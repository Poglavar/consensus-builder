Development Roadmap

What's been built so far, what's in development, and what's the vision for the future.

Oct 2024
Basic Parcel Ingest and Visualization
A system to find and ingest geodata for various cities of the world.Interactive map with parcel boundaries, clickable parcels showing detailed information including area, estimated market price, and geometric analysis.
Completed
Nov 2024
Road Detection & Management
Automatic road detection from OpenStreetMap data and other public sources, manual road drawing tools, road width analysis. Government road plan integration.
Completed
Nov 2024
Introduce Parcel Blocks
Algorithms to automatically group parcels into blocks based on road boundaries. Allow the creation of block proposals (urban rules, squares, buildings, parks...)
Completed
Dec 2024
Proposal System
Complete proposal creation and management system with multi-parcel selection, proposal creation, visualization, storage. Basic proposal types supported.
Completed
Feb 2025
Proposal Sharing & Export
Support different types of proposal sharing: share proposals via URL-encoded data, save data as JSON, upload to server and share a link.
Completed
June 2025
Blockchain integration
Write smart contracts, deploy them to blockchain, make it possible to connect to UI with a blockchain wallet and load parcel and proposal data from the blockchain. Make it possible to mint proposals as NFTs on the blockchain.
Completed
Dec 2025
Additional proposal types, additional proposal features and additional cities support
Add support for additional proposal types, additional proposal features and additional cities support. Add support for Buenos Aires, Argentina, and Belgrade, Serbia.
In Progress
Dec 2025
Rework the road/track proposal system
Rework the road/track proposal system to support robust thousand-parcel-level proposals with site upload and sharing.
In Progress
Feb 2026
Deeper integration with Attestify.Network
Integrate more with the Attestify.Network to make real claims on proposals viable, and thus creating proposals more attractive.
Planned
Mar 2026
Additional cities support, marketing and community building
Roll out support for additional cities to the system. Build a community around the project, market to potential users.
Planned
Jun 2026
AI-aided urban coordination
AI-aided urban development, especially for places where cadastral data is not available. AI-aided detection of existing parcels, roads and buildings. AI-aided proposal creation and evaluation. Autonomous AI agents for proposal opportunity discovery.
Planned
