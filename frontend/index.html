About

This application helps communities reach consensus on future development of their settlement. This is an early alpha version. Do play around freely but do not put in too much work -- all data is saved locally in your browser and will be lost. See the roadmap for the development plan.

Instructions

A key concept is a Parcel Block. It consists of one or more parcels separated from the rest by roads (of any width or type). Each parcel block can contain a Building Block, or be earmarked for some other purpose. Parcel Blocks already exist naturally, every parcel is part of a block of parcels if you go far enough to find the roads that surround them. But the problem is typically that they are too large and badly formed. This application allows you to add new roads to the map (either by marking a parcel as "is road" using the checkbox, or by drawing roads manually) and thus reconfigure the map's Parcel Blocks. Click on "Form blocks" (a slow operation) to recalculate them. You can then propose different structures on blocks.
